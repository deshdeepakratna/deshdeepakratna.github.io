# Neural Circuit Plasticity

## Scientific Question

How does stress reshape neural circuits that regulate motivation, reward, and emotional behavior?

---

## Background

Stress induces persistent adaptations across cortico-basal ganglia circuits. Understanding these circuit-level changes is critical for identifying mechanisms underlying stress susceptibility and resilience.

My research focuses on identifying how synaptic and circuit plasticity contribute to maladaptive behavioral responses following stressful experiences.
