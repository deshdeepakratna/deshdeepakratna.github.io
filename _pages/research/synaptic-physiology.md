# Synaptic Physiology

## Scientific Question

How do stressful experiences alter neuronal excitability and synaptic transmission?

---

## Background

Neurons adapt to experience through changes in intrinsic membrane properties and synaptic communication. My research uses whole-cell electrophysiology to characterize these cellular adaptations.

---

## Current Research

- Intrinsic excitability
- Synaptic plasticity
- Ion channel regulation
- Experience-dependent adaptation

---

## Experimental Techniques

- Whole-cell patch clamp
- Voltage clamp
- Current clamp
- Electrophysiology analysis

---

## Future Directions

Integrating cellular physiology with systems neuroscience to understand behavioral adaptation.
