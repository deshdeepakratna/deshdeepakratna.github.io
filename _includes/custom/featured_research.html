Research

My research integrates systems neuroscience, electrophysiology, advanced imaging, and computational approaches to understand how stress reshapes neural circuits and behavior.

  • Neural Circuit Plasticity – Understanding how stressful experiences remodel basal ganglia circuits that regulate motivation, reward, and emotional behavior.
  • Synaptic Physiology – Investigating neuronal excitability, synaptic transmission, and experience-dependent plasticity using whole-cell electrophysiology.
  • Open & Computational Neuroscience – Developing reproducible software, quantitative analysis pipelines, and open-source tools for modern neuroscience research.
